About Us

Our Story

Resurrection Kitchen was born from a passion for sustainable food practices and community building. We believe in creating nourishing meals that are good for both people and the planet.

Our Vision

We strive to source local, organic ingredients whenever possible, supporting local farmers and reducing our environmental footprint. Our Neighbourhood Shop extends this vision by offering sustainable products for your home.

Our Community

More than just a restaurant and shop, we're a community hub where neighbors can gather, connect, and share experiences around good food and sustainable living.
